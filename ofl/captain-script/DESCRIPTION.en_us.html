Captain Script is a clean and modern handwriting font based on the natural style of its creator, designed to bring a personal and approachable touch to both digital and print projects.

It maintains the flow and warmth of real handwriting while keeping legibility high at both small and large sizes. The font is ideal for quotes, personal notes, invitations, branding, and any project that benefits from a friendly, human touch.

Designed by Suneel D.K in 2025.
